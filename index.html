Info

InfoVizl+Lab is a research team led by Professor Kim Minjung of the New Media Design Department at Hanyang Cyber University. It serves as an interdisciplinary hub dedicated to the academic advancement of information design and data visualization, sharing the latest research, papers, educational resources, and promotional materials. The primary objective of the lab is to develop and research innovative methodologies and approaches for interactive, exploratory, and explanatory data visualizations across areas such as information design, data visualization, data humanism, new media design, and immersive environments. Through this, the lab aims to facilitate the effective communication of data and information, assisting various stakeholders in easily gaining insights from data.
